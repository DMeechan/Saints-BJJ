When do we train?

Right now we have two classes: a no gi class with the MMA club every Thursday, and a gi class every Saturday!

Thursdays at 17:00 in the Sports Hall

BJJ no gi classes with the MMA club

Saturdays at 18:30 in the Sports Hall

BJJ gi classes (recommended for beginners!)

How do I join?

We're super excited to being BJJ to St Andrews! Right now we're collaborating with the MMA club to make this happen.

Come along on Saturday to try out BJJ - it's free to try!

If you enjoy our BJJ classes, sign up for membership with the Saints MMA club.

One Saints MMA membership will give you access to both BJJ classes and MMA classes!

What should I bring?

  • A gi would be super handy for the gi classes on Saturdays, but don't worry if you don't have one! T-shirt and shorts (longsleeved if possible) are okay.
  • A water bottle
  • A mouth guard / gum shield (you can find them on Amazon)
  • Make sure you can get into the sports centre. Please sign up for MMA club membership if you'd like to keep doing BJJ.

Why do I need MMA club membership?

Building the first BJJ club in St Andrews is a tricky task! Thankfully, the Saints MMA club has been kind enough to collaborate with us to help us make this happen.

Signing up for Saints MMA membership gives you access to both BJJ and MMA classes!

To become affiliated with the Athletic Union, we need to demonstrate how many people would love to see BJJ come to St Andrews.

If you have two minutes, please help grow the BJJ club by filling out the short form linked below and liking our Facebook page.
